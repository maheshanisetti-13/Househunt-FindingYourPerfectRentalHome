# Define Problem Statement

In modern urban environments, finding a suitable rental home has become
increasingly complex and time-consuming. Tenants often depend on brokers,
social media posts, or unverified advertisements to search for rental properties.
This results in incomplete information, lack of transparency, and increased
risk of fraud.

Physical visits to multiple properties consume significant time and effort,
especially when details such as rent, amenities, and availability are not
clearly disclosed in advance. Communication gaps between tenants and property
owners further delay the decision-making process.

## Problems Identified
- No centralized platform for rental listings
- Dependence on brokers and intermediaries
- Fake or unverified property advertisements
- Lack of transparency in pricing and amenities
- Time-consuming manual search process

## Need for the System
There is a strong need for a centralized digital platform that allows users to
search, filter, and view verified rental properties with complete details.
The **House Hunt** system aims to simplify the rental process by connecting
tenants and property owners directly through a secure and user-friendly web
application.
