# Empathy Map Canvas

The Empathy Map helps understand the thoughts, emotions, and behaviour of users
who are searching for rental homes.

## Thinks
- Wants affordable rental options
- Expects genuine and verified property listings
- Looks for convenience and quick decision-making

## Feels
- Frustrated with brokers and high commissions
- Confused due to inconsistent pricing information
- Worried about fake listings and fraud

## Says
- “I want verified rental homes”
- “The rent should be transparent”
- “I don’t want to depend on brokers”

## Does
- Searches rental homes online
- Compares prices across platforms
- Contacts multiple property owners

## Pain Points
- Fake or misleading listings
- Hidden charges
- Delayed responses from owners
- Lack of clear property details

## Gains
- Easy search and filtering
- Transparent pricing
- Verified listings
- Direct communication with owners
