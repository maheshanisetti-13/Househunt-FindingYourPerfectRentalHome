# Define Problem Statement

In today’s fast-paced digital world, users expect quick and efficient services.
However, traditional ordering systems are often time-consuming, inefficient,
and require physical presence. Customers face difficulties such as long waiting
times, lack of real-time updates, and limited accessibility to services.

Businesses also struggle with managing orders manually, leading to errors,
delays, and poor customer satisfaction. There is no centralized digital platform
that enables users to place orders easily and track them in real time.

## Problem Identification
- Manual and time-consuming ordering process  
- Lack of real-time order tracking  
- Poor user experience  
- Inefficient order management for businesses  

## Need for the System
A digital solution is required to simplify the ordering process, provide
real-time updates, and improve both customer and business efficiency.
The **Order On The Go** system aims to address these challenges.
